Promotion video for SPCA

Client: SPCA
TVC (Storyboard) / Group Project / 2021
While SPCA celebrated the 100th anniversary, they wished to film a video capturing working scenery also to promote their organization. However, preconception of cruralty upon animal euthanaisa, also unpassionated staffs has lead to an negative image of SPCA. Throguh the TVC, we hoped to illustrate the dilemma for caregivers on doing the decision and pain for takign care of illness animals.

We applied emotional approach to illustrate the decision making before euthanasia in order to bring up the message of 'SPCA has been trying to choose the relatively good solutions in the past 100 years.' The story featured a personified illed cat and caregiver from SPCA.
