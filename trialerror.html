SOS! Share our surreal

Client: Trial and Error
Event Campaign / Group Project / 2022
The more the merrier. Watching Youtube could be a group activity also. Targeting 18 to 35 Hong Kong people who are addicted to social media, we hope through this campaign, fans of Trial and Error would share this interesting channel to their friends.

Named SOS! Share our Surreal, audience would need to ask for help from their friends in order to fulfill the tasks within the campaign. Sharing interetsing videos to friends is alike gifting to others, therefore, gift box is chosen to be the event logo. It also represents the mysterious excitement when unboxing.
Awareness: Through movie poster
Activity: Video series which required audience to find friends to unlock different perspective
Retention: "Fireplace gathering forum"

By exposing audience with Trial and Error's movie posters in MTR stations and instagram ads, it is expected that media would promote and spread as Tiral and Error had mentioned their movie plan before.
A psycological quiz of daily situation will be performed for player to identify their character and video perspective. Player will need to choose 5 out of 9 boxes to answer.

Player could only view their character's video unless they applied other characters' code from their friends. Each code could be used 3 times only. To obtain the whole video, player needs to collect all the charater codes to watch the whole story.
Fireplace gathering forums gather players with the same identitdy to discuss and chat. By using other characters' code, players could access other forums without permission to comment (aka CD-ROM).
