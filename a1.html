About

Founded in 1948, the chain store A-1 Bakery from Japan striked for rank A quality food and taste of No.1 with natural and healthy ingredients. The brand aimed to provide high standard service with warmth, handmade style with Japanese insistence. It is systematic and easy to reach for customers to purchase quality passteries and light food easily.

Rebranding

Facing the saturated market, A-1 Bakery as an early joining party, has a relative commerical identitdy which might be less competitve to current individual shops.
The main challege was to evauate A-1 Bakery, make the brand associated with uniqueness and handmade, yet without lossing the Japanese asthetic and professional manner.

Pattern and illustrations

We have special sets of illustration highlighting both our best selling bread and passteries, also illustrations of our employees working in our company’s enviroment. The illustration features softness and happiness of our company. The illustration can be applied on the packaging of the product, regular merches and decorations. The objects inside the pattern could be used individually if needed. Roughness effect is needed when in usage.
